---
name: clinical-anonymization
description: Removes sensitive data from clinical text while preserving meaning. Use for healthcare data.
---

## Instructions

1. Identify sensitive data:
   - Names
   - Dates
   - Locations
   - IDs

2. Replace with placeholders:
   - [NAME]
   - [DATE]
   - [LOCATION]
   - [ID]

3. Preserve clinical meaning

## Output Format

- Anonymized text
