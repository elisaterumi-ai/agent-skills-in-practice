## Instructions

1. Identify the main topic or insight
2. Structure the post with a strong hook
3. Use short paragraphs for readability
4. Add a clear takeaway
5. Optionally include a call to action

## Output Format

- Hook (first line)
- Body (short paragraphs)
- Closing (insight or CTA)
